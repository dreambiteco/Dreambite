Dreambite Collective

Dreambite Collective is made of three queer, writer-producers, Clare Stenning, Katrina Bennett and Lydia Sabatini.

Formed in 2022, we are committed to making form-breaking work in collaborative and non-hierarchical ways that bring to vivid life the manifold experiences of queer female subjectivity. We revel in the strange, the slimy and the dreamy, and like to show characters dealing with real social issues such as addiction, desire and bodily autonomy from unexpected and visceral angles.

Our Writers

Katrina Bennett - Writer/Producer

"give me dreams about aliens give me vagina-munching zombies give me unicorn prongs give me observation fish tanks and all the wacky, weird shit" - LUCID

  • Lucid Camden People's Theatre, 2024
  • Choose Your Fighter Camden People's Theatre, 2022
  • Someone's F***ing With Their Gun Curve Theatre, 2020

KATRINA BENNETT is a writer from Warwickshire, now based in East London. Katrina has an MA in Writing for Stage and Broadcast Media from the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama for which she received a full scholarship. Her debut play Choose Your Fighter, developed with mentorship from Barrel Organ Theatre and funding from Arts Council England and Jerwood Arts New Work Fund, was performed at festivals across the Midlands in 2022, and had a sold-out run at Camden People’s Theatre in May 2023. Her next play, Consumed, co-written and produced with Dreambite Collective had a sold-out run at Camden People’s Theatre in October 2024. Katrina has worked as a Mentor Playwright for the National Theatre on their New Views Programme. She also has a passion for screen, winning the Watersprite Film Festival Screenwriting Competition in 2021.

Lydia Sabatini - Writer/Producer

"nothing's anything no nothing makes any sense at all until I begin to develop the intense desire to eat myself" - You Are What You Eat

  • You Are What You Eat Camden People's Theatre, 2024
  • You Are What You Eat Broadwater Theater, LA, 2024
  • The Light Trail Hope Theatre, 2021

Lydia Sabatini is a London-based playwright originally from Essex. Her work has been shortlisted for the Women’s Prize for playwriting in 2021 and longlisted in 2023. She has participated in the Bush Theatre’s Emerging Writers Group 2021/2, the Traverse Theatre’s Breakthrough Writers: In Residence Programme 2022/3, the Mercury Theatre’s Playwrights Programme 2022/3, the London Library’s Emerging Writers Programme 2023/4, Orange Tree Theatre’s Writers Collective 2023/4, GNR8 Series 6 for LAMDA/Audible 2024 and BOLD Playwrights 2024 and the Royal Court Writer's Group 2025. She is one of the founding members of Dreambite Collective and co-wrote CONSUMED which sold out it's run at Camden People's Theatre in 2024.

Clare Stenning - Writer/Actor/Producer

"I can feel my toes each one wait one two three four five i can feel them all on both feet connected I'm connected it's amazing" - Gummy Gummy

  • Gummy Gummy Camden People's Theatre, 2024
  • Vacillate King Head's Theatre, 2019
  • #Virginie Teatro Rivoli, Turin, 2018

CLARE STENNING is a Franco-British writer and actor. She has an MA in Writing for Stage & Broadcast Media from the Central School of Speech and Drama. Her debut play #Virginie, was devised with Tom Corradini Teatro, then performed at the Teatro Rivoli, in Turin, Italy. Her next play, Vacillate, was staged as a part of the King’s Head Theatre’s Queer Season, in 2019. She has written several short films in French and English, including A Party to Forget, a finalist at the World Monologue Film Festival.
