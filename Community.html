Community

We are a creative organisation working at the intersection of performance, social dialogue, and community engagement.

As a queer, female, non-hierarchical collective, we are committed to fostering community through creativity and inclusivity. We develop bold and innovative artistic work alongside community programmes that bring together artists, researchers and public audiences to explore urgent social questions. Our work focuses particularly on themes such as the climate, queer and gender identity, and mental health.

Through performances, workshops, and public events, Dreambite aims to create spaces where creative practice becomes a tool for reflection, dialogue, and community.
